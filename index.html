About the Event

The Anti Drug Awareness Run is a community initiative aimed at educating society about the dangers of drug abuse and promoting a healthy and disciplined lifestyle.

This run brings together students, professionals, athletes, and citizens of Visakhapatnam to unite for a common cause.

Together we can build a drug-free and healthier future.
